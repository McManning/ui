## Tips and Tricks

- Do focus on spacing and layouts in grayscale first before applying color
- Do follow the 60/30/10 rule (60% primary, 30% secondary, 10% accent)
- Do not use pure grays or blacks. We rarely see pure grays in real life.
- Do use accessible contrast for text
